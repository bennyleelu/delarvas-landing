Brand Do's & Don'ts
✓ Do
  • Use Deep Navy and Ice Blue as the primary color combination
  • Lead with inventory specifics (quantity, model, condition)
  • Maintain wide letter-spacing on DELARVAS wordmark
  • Use Montserrat for all headings and brand touchpoints
  • Write in a direct, factual, confident tone
✗ Don't
  • Use warm colors (orange, red, yellow) as primary palette
  • Use lowercase "delarvas" — always all-caps
  • Use casual or overly promotional language
  • Stretch or distort the wordmark
  • Place the wordmark on busy backgrounds without contrast
